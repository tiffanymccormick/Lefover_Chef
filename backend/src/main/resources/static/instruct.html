Welcome to Leftover Chef!

Always have food that’s about to go bad or ingredients you have no idea what to do with? Leftover Chef is here to help! You can input all leftover products in your fridge and pantry, control whether you want a breakfast, lunch or dinner meal and get a recipe that uses all of your inputted ingredients. If you don’t like the meal generated, click the new meal button. You can also keep track of how much leftover food you’ve saved. Have fun cooking!
