Welcome to Leftover Chef!

Always have food that’s about to go bad or ingredients you have no idea what to do with? Leftover Chef is here to help! You can input all leftover products in your fridge and pantry, and get a recipe that uses all of your inputted ingredients. Leftover Chef will show estimated time, ingredients, and instructions. If you don’t like the meal generated, click the new meal button. You can also keep track of how much leftover food you’ve saved. Have fun cooking!
